The Pivot

The Switch

  • During COVID I built my own PC — and it changed everything. I'd gamed my whole life, so engineering felt surprisingly natural.
  • It changed the lens through which I viewed everything — the internet, devices, cars, anything involving tech. It felt like my eyes had been opened in a way I'd never experienced before.
  • Made the decision to switch halfway through junior year and gave myself two options heading into graduation: find a Software Engineering job within 6 months — or go to medical school. I had all the prerequisites and clinical rounds done either way.
  • Landed a role as an AI Software Engineer at Cognizant — and haven't looked back.
The Ultimatum

6 months to find a SWE job — or medical school. No in between.
